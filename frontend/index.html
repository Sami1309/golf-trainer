Calibrate onset detector

Runs the spectral-flux detector on every labeled file, matches detections to your ground truth within a tolerance window, and sweeps thresholds to find the best. A detection counts as a true positive if it fires within ±tolerance of a labeled shot. Extra detections are false positives; missed labeled shots are false negatives.
